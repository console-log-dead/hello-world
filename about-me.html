About Me

Who am I?

I'm a 19 year old Singaporean currently studying in Singapore Polytechnic (SP), taking the Diploma in Applied AI & Analytics. As a year 3 student, I'm currently interning at Synapxe, Singapore's HealthTech Agency for 1 year.
In my free time, I do photography of all sorts of people and all sorts of things! (Really wish there were more cats for me to take pictures of though...)

What I'm Doing in Academics

I code mainly in Python, and have experience with using SQL for databases and Ansible for automating scripts. My stronghold is in analytics, where I use Python to manipulate and aggregate data, create graphs and use them in Powerpoint slides to explain what they represent.

What I'm Doing in School Activities


I was a Vice-Chairperson for SP Videography Club (SPVC) in AY2024/25. I oversaw the training & logistic departments and worked with other ExCo members to make decisions for the club like buying new equipment and managing the club. I worked on projects for internal and external clients of the club too as a videographer, covering events like SP Arts Fiesta.

I also joined SP ACER in AY2023/2024 and did outreach events for SP to Secondary Schools and became a bus guide for SP's various open houses in 2024 and 2025.

What I'm Doing Outside School

I am an avid photographer, and I usually do portrait photography & street photography in both digital and film formats. I may be suffering from Gear Acquision Syndrome but I promise it's all worth it (trust me). I'm also pretty deep into the ACG community and play games like Honkai: Star Rail and Project Sekai in my spare time in and out of school.
